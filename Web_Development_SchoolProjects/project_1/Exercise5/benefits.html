Benefits of DevOps

The goals of DevOps span the entire delivery pipeline. They include:

  • Speed - Improved deployment frequency.

  • Rapid Delivery - Faster time to market, shortened lead time between fixes.

  • Reliability - Lower failure rate of new releases.
  • Recovery - Faster mean time to recovery (in the event of a new release crashing or otherwise disabling the current system).

  • Scale - Automation and consistency help you manage complex or changing systems efficiently and with reduced risk.

  • Security - Move quickly while retaining control and preserving compliance(automated).
